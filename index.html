Profile

general arrangement & description

I'm an aerospace engineer with hands-on industry experience across the full product lifecycle — CAD design, FEA, vendor sourcing, and DGCA certification of agricultural and testing UAVs.

I'm currently doing my M.Sc. in Aerospace Engineering at Vilnius Tech, researching aircraft glide-path generation from single-frequency GNSS receivers.

Before Lithuania I was a Design Engineer at NextLeap Aeronautics in Bengaluru, taking UAVs from concept through type certification and customer delivery.

On the side I run CrocByte — a one-person studio where I design and publish Android apps and games. Hardware or software, I like building the whole thing.
